❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📌 What is Retro Chess Hub?

Retro Chess Hub is a website dedicated to chess lovers. We provide chess tips, strategies, and online resources in a **1980s-themed** style.

📌 What are the different pages on this site?

  • 🏠 **Home Page:** Introduces the website, chess tips, and latest updates.
  • ℹ️ **About Page:** Explains our mission, why the site exists, and how you can support it.
  • 💰 **Donate Page:** Offers a way to support our website voluntarily.
  • 📞 **Contact Page:** Allows users to email us with queries (we reply within 48 hours).
  • ❓ **F.A.Q Page:** Provides answers to common questions about the website.

📌 How can I donate?

Donating is **completely optional**, and any amount is welcome! Visit our Donate Page for UPI & QR payment options.

📌 Do you offer chess coaching?

We **do not** offer private coaching. However, we provide free chess tips and strategies.

📌 How can I contact you?

We only handle queries via email. Visit our Contact Page for details.

📌 Will there be online chess games on this website?

Yes, we are planning to add a simple **online chess board** soon.

📌 Is this site safe to use?

Yes, Retro Chess Hub is **completely safe**, contains **no login/signup**, and follows strict privacy policies.
